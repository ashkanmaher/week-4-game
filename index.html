Crystals Collector

You will be given a randon number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number you are given! But... You will lose if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Choose wisely!

Each time the game starts, the random number and the values of the crystals will change
